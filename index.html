About

Introducing: Always Awake

British Producer, Artist, and DJ Specialising in Electronic Dance Music Always Awake is a talented British producer, artist, and DJ who is making waves in the world of electronic dance music (EDM). Based in the United Kingdom, Always Awake’s music covers a wide range of sub-genres, including Dn’B, Trance, House, Pop, and more. One of Always Awake’s most recent and popular remixes is the Bodyrox Feat. Luciana – Yeah Yeah (Always Awake 2023 Remix), which garnered over 2,000 hits within just one week of its release and has been supported by Luciana herself.

Always Awake’s skills and success have not gone unnoticed by the music industry’s greats, as he has been taught by two legendary figures in the field: Darren Bayton from Pink Bomb and Ashley Slater from FreakPower. His work has received considerable support from Judge Jules and has also been featured on BBC Radio 1. Additionally, SortaWorks is a YouTube channel that supports and promotes Always Awake’s music. If you’re a fan of EDM and are always on the lookout for fresh new beats, be sure to subscribe to Always Awake’s channel for more exciting content and updates. With his talent and dedication, he’s sure to keep delivering amazing music for years to come.
